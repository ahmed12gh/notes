# Label each bone in the upper limb.
The upper limb is composed of **30 bones**:
- The [[MD/1 year/anatomy/HDTD/Pectoral Girdle|Pectoral Girdle]]: 3 bones
	-  Clavicle
	-  Scapula
- The *arm* : 1 bone   **Humerus**
- The *forearm*: 2 bones
	-  Radius
	-  Ulna
- The *hand*: 27 bones
	-  8 carpal bones
	-  5 metacarpal bones
	-  14 phalanges
		(3 in each finger, 2 in each thumb)
